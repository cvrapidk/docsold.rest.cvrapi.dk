CVR API REST API

Segment API


Search

The following endpoint will be used to make a segmentation search: curl http://rest.cvrapi.dk/v1/dk/search/company



Parameters


life_name
Whole or part of the companyname. Eg.: Erhvervsstyrelsen

life_start
The companies startdate. You can format the search in different ways: year-month-day, year-month or year-year. Eg.: Search for companies started between 2010-2015 - or companies started in October 2015: 2015-10.

life_end
The companies enddate. You can format the search in different ways: year-month-day, year-month or year-year. Eg.: Search for companies closed between 2010-2015 - or companies closed in October 2015: 2015-10.

contact_phone
The companies whole phonenumber.

contact_email
Whole or part of the e-mail.

address_street
Whole or part of the companies address streetname.

address_numberfrom
Whole part of the companies address housenumber.

address_letterfrom
Whole part of the companies address housenumber letter.

address_zipcode
Whole part of the companies zipcode. Can be stated as a range: Eg.: 8000-9000.

address_city
Whole part of the companies address cityname.

industrycode_text
Whole part of the companies industrycodename.

industrycode_code
Whole part of the companies industrycode. Can be stated as a range: Eg.: 812410-821100.

industrycode_text
Whole part of the companies secondary industrycodename.

industrycode_code
Whole part of the companies secondary industrycode. Can be stated as a range: Eg.: 812410-821100.

companyform_code
Whole part of the companies companyform code. Can be stated as a range: Eg.: 80-81.

companyform_description
Whole part of the companies companyform initials: Eg.: ApS, IVS, A/S.

companystatus_code
Whole part of the companies statuscode. See the list of statuscodes. Use the code from the statuscode list to lookup a specific status.

capital_capital
Whole part of the companies capital. Can be stated as a range: Eg.: 1000-2500.

capital_currency
Virksomhedens kapitals valuta. Wholepart of the companies capital currency. E.g.: DKK, EUR.

capital_ipo
Check whether the company is IPO listed. Set to 'true'.
